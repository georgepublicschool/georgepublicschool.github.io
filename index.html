About GPS

George Public School located in Uttarakhand Vidhayli Shiksha Parishad, NimbuWala, Garhi Cantt is a  Co-Educational Secondary School institution affilated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) on Provisional basis since 2006.

The School has been operating officially under the trust/Society. George Public School Shiksha Samiti.

Infrastructure

George Public School is equipped with 19 rooms, labs to conduct practicals in Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science. All the labs are set up with latest technology and have all necessary equipment and requirements. The labs are spacious and have proper sitting arrangements.

We are heading towards smart learning techniques.All classes are having SMART BOARDS to help students learn more.We have separate SPORTS GROUND to conduct various sports activities and a BASKETBALL court inside the Campus. BUS Facility is also provided to the students .
